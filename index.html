Master your narrative for program management and consulting roles

  • 2Describe influencing stakeholders who didn't report to you to achieve a program goal.

STAR-C framework

STAR-C: Situation → Task → Approach → Result → Consulting Insight. Always close with a long-term recommendation.

  • 1
    Turnaround: A federally-funded CHC is losing HRSA funding due to missed targets. Diagnose and create a 90-day turnaround plan.
  • 2
    Market entry: A national nonprofit wants to expand HIV prevention to three new Southern states. Structure the needs assessment and implementation plan.
  • 3
    Process: A state health dept's STI surveillance data has 40% completeness. What's your diagnostic and intervention approach?
  • 4
    Org design: A health org wants to centralize grant management. What structure do you recommend and how do you build buy-in?
Power stories — STAR method

Practice each until it flows naturally in 2–3 minutes. These are drawn directly from your real career.

  • 5

Consulting interview tips
  • →Lead with structure: "I'd approach this in three phases…"
  • →Quantify everything: $2M+, 8 databases, 7 contractors, 12 programs, $200K budgets.
  • →Your federal fluency — CDC, HRSA, SAMHSA — is your differentiator. Name it explicitly.
  • →Close every case answer with a consulting insight and long-term recommendation.
  • →Your DHA is your edge — weave implementation science language in naturally.
  • →Pause before answering to gather your thoughts. Silence reads as confidence.
